## 💡 Pro Tips for Filming
1. **Lighting:** Film in a dark room with one blue or purple LED strip in the background to hit that "Cyberpunk/Lab" aesthetic.
2. **Stability:** Use a tripod or lean your phone against something. No shaky footage.
3. **Pacing:** Every cut should be fast (less than 3 seconds per scene).
4. **Text:** Use the "Classic" or "System" font style in TikTok/Instagram to keep it looking technical.
